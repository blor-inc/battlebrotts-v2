# Audio Vision

*Captured from Creative Director (Eric) + The Bott conversation, 2026-04-16.*
*This is the creative north star for all audio work. Sprint Gizmo/Nutts reference this before touching audio.*

## The Vision (One Paragraph)

BattleBrotts is charming, sharp, and sincere — like WALL-E. Brotts have soul. Players should say "my brott" with affection and root for the underdog. Audio is thoughtful and sparse, not hype or slapstick. Music is an orchestral-electronic hybrid (Kena meets Thomas Newman) that carries emotional weight. SFX are mechanical and earnest — servos, whirs, handmade weapons — with punchy moments for impact. BrottBrain speaks in WALL-E chirps with text, personality in pitch and rhythm. UI is split: workshop is hands-on mechanical; menus and shop are warm chimes. Victory and defeat are character-driven — the brott itself reacts with proud or sad chirps, music supports with earned warmth. No punishment, only pathos. Players *like* their brott.

## Tone

- **Charming, sharp, sincere** — WALL-E as primary reference
- Goofy energy on a polished foundation — players smile, they don't wince
- Slapstick is too much of a joke; hype announcer is too strong
- Brotts are buddies, not cannon fodder

## Energy

- Thoughtful and sparse — not constant hype
- Every sound means something
- Music does the emotional lifting

## BrottBrain Voice

- **Primary:** WALL-E-style chirps and beeps with text on screen showing the trick
- Pitch and rhythm carry mood — curious chirp for cautious tricks, confident warble for aggressive ones
- Feels like a co-pilot, not a narrator
- **Fallback:** Subtle musical motifs (different moods) or a signature chime if chirps get annoying in practice

## Music

- Orchestral-electronic hybrid
- Warm strings, playful woodwinds layered with atmospheric synths
- Emotional weight, slightly magical
- References: Kena: Bridge of Spirits, Thomas Newman (WALL-E, Finding Nemo)
- Music carries the emotional lift — brotts feel loved, not mocked

## Combat SFX

- **Foundation:** Mechanical & earnest — servos, whirs, pneumatics, sparks, clunks
- Shots sound handmade and bolted-together, not AAA guns
- **Big moments** (railgun, KO, shield break): punchy oomph layered in for impact
- Restraint 90% of the time so the big moments land

## UI SFX

- **Workshop / equip screens:** mechanical clicks — toolbox, gears, toggle switches. Feels hands-on.
- **Menus / shop / card selection:** soft warm chimes, gentle swooshes. Feels welcoming.
- Each area has its own audio texture matching what the player is doing.

## Victory & Defeat

- **Character-driven:** The brott itself reacts with chirps — proud warble on wins, sad descending chirp on losses
- The brott is the star; music supports
- Warm, earned musical flourish on wins (not triumphant orchestral hit)
- Losses get gentle, compassionate tone — "that was a good try, buddy"
- **No punishment on losses — only pathos**

## Core Principle

**Players should feel affection for their brott no matter the outcome.**
