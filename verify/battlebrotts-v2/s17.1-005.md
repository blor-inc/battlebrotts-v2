## 7. Critical invariant — preview ≡ commit

Structural proof:

1. `shop_screen._maybe_show_trick_then_build()` calls `_prepare_trick_for_modal(trick)`, which **deep-duplicates** the trick dict and pre-resolves any `random_*` ITEM tokens to concrete direct tokens (`resolved["token"]`).
2. That single `patched` dict is passed to `modal.show_trick(patched)`.
3. The modal's `_populate_previews` reads `patched.choice_a / choice_b` and calls `_name_from_token(ev)` — which is `ItemTokens.display_name(ItemTokens.resolve_token(ev))` on the already-resolved direct token (idempotent at that point).
4. On resolution, the **same** `patched` dict is then passed to `game_state.apply_trick_choice(patched, choice_key)`.

There is no second RNG draw, no second resolve, no re-randomization between preview and commit. Preview name and commit name necessarily come from the identical token string. Invariant: **HOLDS by construction.**
